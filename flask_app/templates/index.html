We are a team of developers and researchers dedicated to streamlining international academic recognition. This project leverages natural language processing (NLP), optical character recognition (OCR), and fuzzy matching techniques to help students quickly identify their institutions and match them to global university rankings.

Originally developed as part of a final-year Master's project in Business Analytics at Trinity College Dublin, this tool was designed to support the student recruitment team by accelerating the process of extracting and verifying university names against an existing rankings dataset. Our broader goal is to enhance transparency and accessibility for applicants pursuing academic or professional opportunities worldwide.
